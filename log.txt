        <p><span><img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/runs-retail + classic-brightgreen" alt="" width="126" height="20"><a href="https://www.patreon.com/jaliborc" rel="nofollow">&nbsp;<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/donate-patreon-orange" alt="" width="100" height="20"></a>&nbsp;<a href="https://github.com/tullamods/Wildpants/wiki" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/read-wiki-blue" alt="" width="66" height="20"></a></span></p>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Bagnon is a highly customizable bag replacement addon designed to help the player find items as quickly and as easily as possible. Beyond the basic all-bags-in-one functionality, Bagnon provides features such as:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to view the items of any character, from anywhere <span style="color:#a9a9a9"><span style="font-size:80.0%">*</span></span></li>
<li>Inventory, bank, vault and guild bank support</li>
<li>Coloring based on item quality and more</li>
<li>Intelligent item search engine</li>
<li>Tooltip item counts</li>
<li>Item rulesets</li>
<li>Databroker support</li>
</ul>
<p><span style="color:#a9a9a9"><span style="font-size:75.0%">*&nbsp;<em>- Requires the player to login and visit the bank once at each character</em></span></span></p>
<h2>Classic Look?</h2>
<p>Looking for a classic look? Try&nbsp;<a href="https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/combuctor" rel="nofollow">Combuctor</a>, which is the same addon but with a more Blizzard looking design.</p>
<h2>Plugins</h2>
<p>A wide assortment of plugins has been made by people in the community:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://wow.curseforge.com/projects/bagnon-currencies" rel="nofollow">Bagnon Currencies</a>&nbsp;- Allows you to track currencies on any window</li>
<li><a href="https://wow.curseforge.com/projects/bagnon-scrap" rel="nofollow">Bagnon Scrap</a>&nbsp;- Colors your Scrap on all windows</li>
<li><a href="https://wow.curseforge.com/projects/bagnon-facade" rel="nofollow">Bagnon Facade</a>&nbsp;- Allows windows to be skinned with Masque</li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/tullamods/Wildpants/wiki/Plugins" rel="nofollow">And many more...</a></li>
</ul>
